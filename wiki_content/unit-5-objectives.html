Successful completion of this unit will enable you to:

  • Identify the relative percentages of salt water vs. freshwater and the various sources of freshwater on Earth.
  • Describe the water cycle.
  • Describe the physical features of the freshwater systems of streams, lakes, and wetlands.
  • Describe the importance of freshwater as a resource.
  • Summarize the glaciation history of the Earth and describe the effects glaciers have on the topography of Earth from formation to movement and through melting.
