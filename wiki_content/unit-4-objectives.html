Successful completion of this unit will enable you to:

  • Explain the presence of mountains at all three types of plate boundaries.
  • Describe how the formation of mountains affects climate, weather, and plant and animal habitats.
  • Describe, compare and contrast the processes of weathering and erosion.
  • Classify types of weathering as either chemical, mechanical, or both.
  • Describe soil, its components, and its layers called soil horizons.
