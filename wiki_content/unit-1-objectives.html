Successful completion of this unit will enable you to:

  • Explain how the Earth can be viewed as a system and a group of systems.
  • Compare the layers of Earth in terms of physical characteristics.
  • Discuss and give examples of physical properties of minerals.
  • Describe the geologic products that result from mechanical and chemical weathering.
  • Discuss and give examples of the physical properties of the three main categories of rocks and the processes that produced them.
