Getting the Course Text

Step 1: Register for Your e-Textbook Course

Go to account.cengage.com to start the registration process.

Already have a Cengage account?

  1. Sign-in now.
    *I    f you’ve used MindTap, CengageNOWv2, OWLv2, SAM, WebAssign or another Cengage platform in a different course, you might already have a Cengage account.

New to Cengage?

  1.  Click “Create Student Account” and register. We recommend using your school email.
  2. If you've just created a new account, open the ‘Welcome to Cengage’ email you just received and click ‘Activate Cengage Account’ to set your password and complete the registration process.
    *Be sure to use the same device and browser to create and verify your account.
  3. Linking your accounts is a one-time process. If you’ve previously completed this step for another course, you won’t be prompted to do it again.
  4. You’ve found your eTextbook!
    • If you purchased an access code, click “Enter your access code” to redeem it.
    • If you don’t have an access code, click “Continue.”

Step 2: Purchase the E-Textbook

You have two options for purchasing your eTextbook.

Option 1: Purchase the eTextbook for just this course.

  • Click “BUY NOW” to purchase access to the eTextbook you need for this course.
  • If you already redeemed an access code, click “Continue” to get started.

Option 2: Purchase a Cengage Unlimited Plan

  • If you have multiple courses that use Cengage materials, you can save money through a Cengage Unlimited plan,.
  • For more information, review the Cengage Unlimited plan options.
