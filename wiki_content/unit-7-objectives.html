Successful completion of this unit will enable you to:

  • Summarize the composition and evolution of the atmosphere 4.6 billion years ago to 2.6 billion years ago.
  • Explain the relationship of geography to temperature.
  • Discuss the use of the term "greenhouse gas" in relation to the Earth's atmosphere.
  • Describe the formation and intensity of winds near the Earth's surface and the three ways that air can be lifted.
  • Compare the different types of precipitation and describe the formation of the three main types of clouds.
