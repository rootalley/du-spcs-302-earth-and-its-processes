Successful completion of this unit will enable you to:

  • Summarize the characteristics of deserts and explain the reasons for their existence.
  • Describe the effect of the oceans on global climate.
  • Discuss the location of the world's oceans in relation to the density of the Earth's crust and describe the faulting that occurs in the ocean basins.
  • Describe the Coriolis effect and the relationship between surface and deep water currents and climate.
