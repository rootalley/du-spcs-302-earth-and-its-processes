After you have finished watching "How the Earth Was Made," respond to the following writing prompts using complete sentences and proper grammar.

  1. In your own words, briefly describe how they now believe the Earth was first formed.
  2. In your own words, briefly describe how that conglomeration of space rocks became the Earth as it is described in the text with a molten core, and various layers leading upward to the mantle and Earth's crust.
  3. Explain why early rocks are hard to find.
  4. Describe the method C.C. Patterson used to determine the age of the Earth; and indicate the age that was determined.
  5. Explain why are early rocks are hard to find.
  6. Describe Hartman's theory on how the moon was formed.
  7. Describe how and where the oldest identifiable rocks were found on the Earth's surface.
  8. In the oldest rocks, describe the evidence found regarding the Earth's early crust and the presence of oceans.
  9. Briefly describe how water came to be on the Earth.
  10. In your own words, briefly describe how amino acids—the building blocks of life—came to be on Earth.
